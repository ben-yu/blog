Hearing Diagnostic App    An iOS play-based audiometric hearing diagnostic app



I am currently working on an app that allows the user to play a game with acoustic ques which first diagnoses the hearing ability of the user and potentially enables the user to overcome their hearing issues by better adapting to the game. This project is part of ongoing research at APL

Battery Sorter    Fully-Autonomous Battery Sorter

– Designed a fully autonomous robot to sort batteries based on size and charge.

– Fabricated essential circuitry including an H-Bridge motor driver, stepper motor driver, infrared sensors, and power supply

– Produced full 3D mock-up of machine in Google SketchUp

TTC Signage Proposal    Updating TTC signage to improve usability for visual/hearing impaired

- Identified signage problems in the TTC, and proposed improvements such as: strategic sign placement, improved color contrast, tactile feedback system on handrails & floor tiles, and consistent font selection

- Produced scale mock-up of St. George Station with proposed signage changes

- Created surveying program to evaluate our new system compared to current signage system

Bridge Design     Designed a pedestrian bridge & a scaled mock-up cardboard bridge

- Collaborated with 3 team members to design and build a model railway bridge that would safely support the passage of a 400N train, while restricted to using one sheet of mat-board 32" x 40" x 0.05" and contact cement

- Final bridge design withstood approximately 500N of force

- Design accounted for axial and flexural stresses (compressive and tensile), shear stress, beam deflection, & plate buckling - Wrote 12 page engineering design report explaining our design process, justifying our design decisions and provided grounds in the form of calculations and drawings
